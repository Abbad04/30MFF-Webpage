A Trip to the Beach

more than just a journey

Skateboarding Seaside

A year ago, I relocated to Abu Dhabi to pursue my college education, and I quickly discovered a favorite local pastime: visiting the beach. However, a new habit has since taken shape—skateboarding to the beach. What started as a mere means of transport has evolved into the highlight of my beach trips. Cruising on my skateboard, with the warm breeze and the scenic coastal route, has unexpectedly become the best part of my beach outings.

The Trip

On my skateboard rides to the beach, the journey itself unfolds as a vivid tableau of experiences. Gliding along the highway, I'm embraced by the fresh breeze, enhancing the exhilarating sense of speed beneath my feet. Each push propels me past scenes where the wild beauty of gazelles complements the sprawling coastline. The rhythmic pulse of my favorite music syncs with the scenery, crafting a dynamic backdrop to these moments of swift movement and serene observation. It’s these details that transform every skateboard trip into a cherished adventure.
